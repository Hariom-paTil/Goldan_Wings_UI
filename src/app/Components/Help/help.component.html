Help & Support

Getting Started

  • Browse our delicious cake collection in the Cakes menu
  • Use Customize to design your own custom cake
  • Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  • Create an account or checkout as a guest

How to Order

  1. Select your favorite cake or create a custom design
  2. Choose size, flavor, and toppings
  3. Add to cart and review your order
  4. Proceed to checkout and enter delivery details
  5. Complete payment to confirm your order

Account Management

  • Click the avatar icon to access your account menu
  • Sign up to save your preferences and order history
  • Track your orders in the order confirmation page
  • Use logout to securely exit your account

Custom Cakes

  • Upload a cake design image for reference
  • Specify size, flavors, and dietary requirements
  • Leave special instructions for our bakers
  • Contact us for bulk orders or special occasions

Contact Us

For additional support, visit our Contacts page or reach out to our customer service team.
